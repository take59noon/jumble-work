### 基本式  
$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = \big| \overrightarrow{a}\big| \big| \overrightarrow{b}\big| \cos\theta$$
$$0{^\circ} \leqq \theta \leqq 180{^\circ}$$
<br>

$$\overrightarrow{a} = \left( a_{1},\ a_{2},\ a_{3} \right),\ \overrightarrow{b} = \left( b_{1},\ b_{2},\ b_{3} \right)$$
$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = a_{1}b_{1} + a_{2}b_{2} + a_{3}b_{3}$$
$$\cos\theta = \dfrac{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b}}{\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big| \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|}$$
<br>

$$\overrightarrow{a}\bot\overrightarrow{b}\enspace \Longleftrightarrow \enspace \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = 0 \enspace \Longleftrightarrow \enspace a_{1}b_{1} + a_{2}b_{2} + a_{3}b_{3} = 0$$

### 計算式の例

$1$ 辺の長さが $2$ の正四面体 ${\rm OABC}$ において，辺 ${\rm BC}$ の中点を ${\rm M}$ とする．  

$$\big| \overrightarrow{\rm OA} \big| = \big| \overrightarrow{\rm OB} \big| = \big| \overrightarrow{\rm OC} \big| = 2$$
$$\overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OB} = \overrightarrow{\rm OB} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OC} = \overrightarrow{\rm OC} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OA} = 2$$
$$\overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OM} = \overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \dfrac{\overrightarrow{\rm OB} + \overrightarrow{\rm OC}}{2} = \dfrac{1}{2}\big( \overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OB} + \overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OC} \big) = 2$$
<br>

$$\big| \overrightarrow{\rm OM} \big|^{2} = \left| \dfrac{\overrightarrow{\rm OB} + \overrightarrow{\rm OC}}{2} \right|^{2} = \dfrac{1}{4}\Big( \big| \overrightarrow{\rm OB} \big|^{2} + 2\overrightarrow{\rm OB} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OC} + \big| \overrightarrow{\rm OC} \big|^{2} \Big) = 3$$
$$\big| \overrightarrow{\rm OM} \big| = \sqrt{3}$$
$$\cos{\angle{\rm AOM}} = \dfrac{\overrightarrow{\rm OA} \cdot \overrightarrow{\rm OM}}{\big| \overrightarrow{\rm OA}\big| \big| \overrightarrow{\rm OM} \big|} = \dfrac{2}{2 \times \sqrt{3}} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}$$
<br>

$\overrightarrow{\rm OA} = \overrightarrow{a}$，$\overrightarrow{\rm OB} = \overrightarrow{b}$，$\overrightarrow{\rm OC} = \overrightarrow{c}$，$\overrightarrow{\rm OM} = \overrightarrow{m}$  

$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big| = \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big| = \big| \overrightarrow{c} \big| = 2 \qquad \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = \overrightarrow{b} \cdot \overrightarrow{c} = \overrightarrow{c} \cdot \overrightarrow{a} = 2$$
$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{m} = \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \dfrac{\overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{c}}{2} = \dfrac{1}{2}\big( \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{c} \big) = 2$$
$$\big| \overrightarrow{m} \big|^{2} = \left| \dfrac{\overrightarrow{b} + \overrightarrow{c}}{2} \right|^{2} = \dfrac{1}{4}\Big( \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} + 2\overrightarrow{b} \cdot \overrightarrow{c} + \big| \overrightarrow{c} \big|^{2} \Big) = 3 \qquad \big| \overrightarrow{m} \big| = \sqrt{3}$$
$$\cos{\angle{\rm AOM}} = \dfrac{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{m}}{\big| \overrightarrow{a}\big| \big| \overrightarrow{m} \big|} = \dfrac{2}{2 \times \sqrt{3}} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}$$
