### 基本式  

$$\alpha = a + bi$$
<br>

$3$ 点 $0$，$\alpha$，$\beta$ が一直線上にある  
$\Longleftrightarrow$ $\ \beta = k\alpha$ となる実数 $k$ がある  
<br>

$$\overline{\alpha} = a - bi$$

$\alpha$ が実数 $\ \Longleftrightarrow$ $\ \overline{\alpha} = \alpha$  

$\alpha$ が純虚数 $\ \Longleftrightarrow$ $\ \overline{\alpha} = -\alpha,\enspace \alpha \neq 0$  

$$\overline{\alpha + \beta} = \overline{\alpha} + \overline{\beta}$$
$$\overline{\alpha - \beta} = \overline{\alpha} - \overline{\beta}$$
<br>

$$\overline{\alpha\beta} = \overline{\alpha}\overline{\beta}$$
$$\overline{\left( \dfrac{\alpha}{\beta} \right)} = \dfrac{\overline{\alpha}}{\overline{\beta}}$$
<br>

$$|\alpha| = |a + bi| = \sqrt{a^{2} + b^{2}}$$
$$\left| \alpha^{2} \right| = \alpha\overline{\alpha}$$
$$|\alpha| = | - \alpha| = \left| \overline{\alpha} \right|$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
|\alpha - \beta|^{2} 
&= (\alpha - \beta)\overline{(\alpha - \beta)} \\
&= (\alpha - \beta)( \overline{\alpha} - \overline{\beta} ) \\
&= \alpha\overline{\alpha} - \alpha\overline{\beta} - \overline{\alpha}\beta + \beta\overline{\beta} \\
&= |\alpha|^{2} - \alpha\overline{\beta} - \overline{\alpha}\beta + |\beta|^{2}
\end{aligned}$$
