### 基本式

$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = \big| \overrightarrow{a} \big|\big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|\cos\theta$$
$$0{^\circ} \leqq \theta \leqq 180{^\circ}$$
<br>

$$\overrightarrow{a} = \left( a_{1},\enspace a_{2} \right),\enspace \overrightarrow{b} = \left( b_{1},\enspace b_{2} \right)$$
$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = a_{1}b_{1} + a_{2}b_{2}$$
$$\cos\theta = \dfrac{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b}}{\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big|\big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|}$$
<br>

#### 垂直・平行  

$$\overrightarrow{a}\bot\overrightarrow{b} \ \Longleftrightarrow \ \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = 0 \  \Longleftrightarrow \ a_{1}b_{1} + a_{2}b_{2} = 0$$
$$\overrightarrow{a}{\verb!∥!}\overrightarrow{b} \ \Longleftrightarrow \ a_{1}b_{1} - a_{2}b_{2} = 0$$
<br>

#### その他

$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{a} = \big| \overrightarrow{a} \big|^{2}$$
$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big| = \sqrt{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{a}}$$
$$-\big|\overrightarrow{a}\big| \big|\overrightarrow{b}\big| \leqq \overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} \leqq \big|\overrightarrow{a}\big| \big|\overrightarrow{b}\big|$$
<br>

### 計算式の例  

$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big| = 2,\enspace \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big| = 6,\enspace \big| \overrightarrow{a} - \overrightarrow{b} \big| = 2\sqrt{7}$$
<br>

$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} - \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} = \big| \overrightarrow{a} \big|^{2} - 2\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} + \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2}$$
$$\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} = \dfrac{1}{2}\left( \big|\overrightarrow{a} \big|^{2} + \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} - \big| \overrightarrow{a} - \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} \right) = \dfrac{1}{2} \times \left\{ 2^{2} + 6^{2} - ( 2\sqrt{7} )^{2} \right\} = 6$$
<br>

$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} + \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} = \big| \overrightarrow{a} \big|^{2} + 2\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b} + \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|^{2} = 2^{2} + 2 \times 6 + 6^{2} = 52$$
$$\big| \overrightarrow{a} + \overrightarrow{b} \big| = 2\sqrt{13}$$
<br>

$$\cos\theta = \dfrac{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot \overrightarrow{b}}{\big| \overrightarrow{a} \big| \big| \overrightarrow{b} \big|} = \dfrac{6}{2 \times 6} = \dfrac{1}{2}$$
$0{^\circ} \leqq \theta \leqq 180{^\circ}$ より，$\theta = 60{^\circ}$
