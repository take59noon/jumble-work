### 基本式

$$f'(x) = \lim_{h \rightarrow 0}\dfrac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h}$$
<br>

$$\left\{ kf(x) + lg(x) \right\}' = kf'(x) + lg'(x)$$
$$\left\{ f(x)g(x) \right\}' = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)$$
$$\left\{ f(x)g(x)h(x) \right\}' = f'(x)g(x)h(x) + f(x)g'(x)h(x) + f(x)g(x)h'(x)$$
$$\left\{ \dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right\}' = \dfrac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{\left\{ g(x) \right\}^{2}}$$
<br>

$$\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{dy}{du} \cdot \dfrac{du}{dx}$$
$$\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{dx}{dy}}$$
<br>

$$\left( x^{p} \right)' = px^{p - 1}$$
<br>

### 計算式の例
* $y = \left( 2x^{2} + 1 \right)^{5}$  

$$y' = 5\left( 2x^{2} + 1 \right)^{4} \cdot \left( 2x^{2} + 1 \right)' = 5\left( 2x^{2} + 1 \right)^{4} \cdot 4x = 20x\left( 2x^{2} + 1 \right)^{4}$$
<br>

* $y = \dfrac{x + 1}{x - 1}$

$$y' = \dfrac{(x + 1)'(x - 1) - (x + 1)(x - 1)'}{(x - 1)^{2}} = \dfrac{x - 1 - (x + 1)}{(x - 1)^{2}} = - \dfrac{2}{(x - 1)^{2}}$$
