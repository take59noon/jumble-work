### 複素数の相等
$$a+bi=c+di \enspace\Longleftrightarrow\enspace \textrm{$a=c$ かつ $b=d$}$$
$$a+bi=0 \enspace\Longleftrightarrow\enspace \textrm{$a=0$ かつ $b=0$}$$
<br>

### 加減乗除
$$\left(a+bi\right)+\left(c+di\right)=\left(a+c\right)+\left(b+d\right)i$$
$$\left(a+bi\right)-\left(c+di\right)=\left(a-c\right)+\left(b-d\right)i$$
$$\left(a+bi\right)\left(c+di\right)=\left(ac-bd\right)+\left(ad+bc\right)i$$
$$\dfrac{a+bi}{c+di}=\dfrac{\left(a+bi\right)\left(c-di\right)}{\left(c+di\right)\left(c-di\right)}=\dfrac{ac+bd}{c^2+d^2}+\dfrac{bc-ad}{c^2+d^2}i$$
<br>

### 計算式の例
$$\left(1+3i\right)\left(2+i\right)=2+7i+3i^2=2+7i-3=-1+7i$$
<br>
$$\left(6-2i\right)\left(6+2i\right)=6^2-4i^2=36+4=40$$
<br>
$$\left(3-2i\right)^2=3^2-12i+4i^2=9-12i-4=5-12i$$
<br>
$$\dfrac{3+i}{3-i}=\dfrac{\left(3+i\right)^2}{\left(3-i\right)\left(3+i\right)}=\dfrac{8+6i}{9+1}=\dfrac{4+3i}{5}=\dfrac{4}{5}+\dfrac{3}{5}i$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\left(2+i\right)^3
&=2^3+3\cdot2^2\cdot i+3\cdot2\cdot i^2+i^3 \cr
&=8+12i+6i^2+i^3 \cr
&=8+12i-6-i \cr
&=2+11i \cr
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\left(\dfrac{3-2i}{2+3i}\right)^2
&=\left\{\dfrac{\left(3-2i\right)\left(2-3i\right)}{\left(2+3i\right)\left(2-3i\right)}\right\}^2 \cr
&=\left(\dfrac{6-13i+6i^2}{4-9i^2}\right)^2 
=\left(\dfrac{-13i}{13}\right)^2 
=\left(-i\right)^2=-1 
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{1-i}{1+i}+\dfrac{1+i}{1-i}
&=\dfrac{\left(1-i\right)^2}{\left(1+i\right)\left(1-i\right)}+\dfrac{\left(1+i\right)^2}{\left(1-i\right)\left(1+i\right)} \cr
&=\dfrac{-2i}{2}+\dfrac{2i}{2} \cr
&=-i+i=0
\end{aligned}$$
