### 変換公式
$$\sin{\left(\theta+2n\pi\right)}=\sin{\theta},\enspace \cos{\left(\theta+2n\pi\right)}=\cos{\theta},\enspace \tan{\left(\theta+2n\pi\right)}=\tan{\theta}$$
$$\sin{\left(-\theta\right)}=-\sin{\theta},\enspace \cos{\left(-\theta\right)}=\cos{\theta},\enspace \tan{\left(-\theta\right)}=-\tan{\theta}$$
$$\sin{\left(\pi\pm\ \theta\right)}=\mp\sin{\theta},\enspace \cos{\left(\pi\pm\ \theta\right)}=-\cos{\theta},\enspace \tan{\left(\pi\pm\ \theta\right)}=\pm\tan{\theta}$$
$$\sin{\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\pm\ \theta\right)}=\cos{\theta},\enspace \cos{\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\pm\ \theta\right)}=\mp\sin{\theta},\enspace \tan{\left(\dfrac{\pi}{2}\pm\ \theta\right)}=\mp\dfrac{1}{\tan{\theta}}$$
<br>

### 三角関数の相互関係
$$\tan{\theta}=\dfrac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}},\enspace \sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}=1,\enspace 1+\tan^2{\theta}=\dfrac{1}{\cos^2{\theta}}$$
<br>

### 式の値
$$2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}=\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right)-\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)^2$$
$$\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)^2=\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}+2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}$$
$$\sin^3{\theta}-\cos^3{\theta}=\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)^3+3\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)$$
$$\sin^3{\theta}-\cos^3{\theta}=\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}+\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}\right)$$
<br>

$\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}=\dfrac{1}{3}$ のとき  
$$\begin{aligned}
2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}
&=\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)^2-\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right) \cr
&=\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^2-1=-\dfrac{8}{9} \\[1em]
\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}&=-\dfrac{4}{9}\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}\tan{\theta}+\dfrac{1}{\tan{\theta}}
&=\dfrac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}}+\dfrac{\cos{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}} \\[.8em]
&=\dfrac{\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}}=\dfrac{1}{\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}}=-\dfrac{9}{4}\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\tan^3{\theta}+\dfrac{1}{\tan^3{\theta}}
&=\left(\tan{\theta}+\dfrac{1}{\tan{\theta}}\right)^3-3\tan{\theta}\cdot\dfrac{1}{\tan{\theta}}\left(\tan{\theta}+\dfrac{1}{\tan{\theta}}\right) \\[.8em]
&=\left(-\dfrac{9}{4}\right)^3-3\cdot\left(-\dfrac{9}{4}\right)=-\dfrac{297}{64}\end{aligned}$$
