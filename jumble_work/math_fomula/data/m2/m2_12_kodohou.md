### 一般角
$$\theta=\alpha+360\degree\times n$$
<br>

### 一般角の三角関数
$$\sin{\theta}=\dfrac{y}{r}$$
$$\cos{\theta}=\dfrac{x}{r}$$
$$\tan{\theta}=\dfrac{y}{x}$$
<br>

### 弧度法
$$180\degree=\pi \ \textrm{ラジアン}$$
<br>

$$\theta=\alpha+2n\pi$$
<br>

### 扇形
$$l=r\theta$$
$$S=\dfrac{1}{2}r^2\theta=\dfrac{1}{2}rl$$

### 計算式の例
$$\sin{\dfrac{2}{3}\pi}=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2},\enspace \cos{\dfrac{2}{3}\pi}=-\dfrac{1}{2},\enspace \tan{\dfrac{2}{3}\pi}=-\sqrt3$$
$$\sin{\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)}=-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt2},\enspace \cos{\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt2},\enspace \tan{\left(-\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)}=-1$$
$$\sin{\left(-\dfrac{19}{6}\pi\right)}=\dfrac{1}{2},\enspace \cos{\left(-\dfrac{19}{6}\pi\right)}=-\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2},\enspace \tan{\left(-\dfrac{19}{6}\pi\right)}=-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt3}$$
<br>
