### 一般形
$$\left(x-a\right)^2+\left(y-b\right)^2=r^2$$
$$x^2+y^2=r^2$$
$$x^2+y^2+lx+my+n=0$$
<br>

### 円の接線
$$x_1 x+y_1 y=r^2$$
$${x_1}^2+{y_1}^2=r^2$$
<br>

### 計算式の例
$$\begin{aligned}
& x^2+y^2-2x+6y+8=0 \cr
& \left(x-1\right)^2-1^2+\left(y+3\right)^2-3^2+8=0 \cr
& \left(x-1\right)^2+\left(y+3\right)^2=2 
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& x^2+y^2+4x-7y+10=0 \cr
& \left(x+2\right)^2-2^2+\left(y-\dfrac{7}{2}\right)^2-\left(\dfrac{7}{2}\right)^2+10=0 \cr
& \left(x+2\right)^2+\left(y-\dfrac{7}{2}\right)^2=\dfrac{25}{4} \cr
\end{aligned}$$
