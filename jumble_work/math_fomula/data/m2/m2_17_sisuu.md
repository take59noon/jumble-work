### 基本式
$$a^0=1$$
$$a^{\frac{m}{n}}=\sqrt[n]{a^m}=\left(\sqrt[n]{a}\right)^m$$
$$a^{-r}=\dfrac{1}{a^r}$$
<br>

$$a^r a^s=a^{r+s}$$
$$(a^{r})^s=a^{rs}$$
$$(ab)^r=a^r b^r$$
<br>

$$\left(\sqrt[n]{a}\right)^n=a$$
$$\sqrt[n]{a}\sqrt[n]{b}=\sqrt[n]{ab}$$
$$\dfrac{\sqrt[n]{a}}{\sqrt[n]{b}}=\sqrt[n]{\dfrac{a}{b}}$$
<br>
$$\left(\sqrt[n]{a}\right)^m=\sqrt[n]{a^m}$$
$$\sqrt[m]{\sqrt[n]{a}}=\sqrt[mn]{a}$$
$$\sqrt[n]{a^m}=\sqrt[np]{a^{mp}}$$
<br>

### 計算式の例
$$2^5\times2^{-3}=2^{5-3}=2^2=4$$
$$\left(6^2\right)^4\div6^7=6^{8-7}=6^1=6$$
<br>

$${16}^{-\frac{3}{4}}
=\left(2^4\right)^{-\frac{3}{4}}
=2^{4\times\left(-\frac{3}{4}\right)}
=2^{-3}=\dfrac{1}{8}$$
$$\sqrt[3]{3}\times\sqrt[6]{3}\div\sqrt3
=3^\frac{1}{3}\times3^\frac{1}{6}\div3^\frac{1}{2}
=3^{\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{6}-\frac{1}{2}}
=3^0=1$$
<br>

$$\left(a^\frac{1}{3}+b^\frac{1}{3}\right)\left(a^\frac{2}{3}-a^\frac{1}{3}b^\frac{1}{3}+b^\frac{2}{3}\right)=a+b$$
<br>

$2^x+2^{-x}=3$ のとき  
$$\begin{aligned}
2^{2x}+2^{-2x}
&=(2^x)^2+(2^{-x})^2 \cr
&=(2^x+2^{-x})^2-2\cdot 2^x\cdot 2^{-x} \cr
&=3^2-2=7
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
2^{3x}+2^{-3x}
&=(2^x)^3+(2^{-x})^3 \cr
&=(2^x+2^{-x})\left\{(2^x)^2-2^x\cdot 2^{-x}+(2^{-x})^2\right\} \cr
&=(2^x+2^{-x})(2^{2x}+2^{-2x}-1) \cr
&=3\cdot\left(7-1\right)=18
\end{aligned}$$
