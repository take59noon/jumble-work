### 基本形
$$ax^2+bx+c=a\left(x-\alpha\right)\left(x-\beta\right)$$
$$\alpha+\beta=-\dfrac{b}{a}$$
$$\alpha\beta=\dfrac{c}{a}$$
<br>

### 頻出表現
$$\alpha^2\beta+\alpha\beta^2=\alpha\beta\left(\alpha+\beta\right)$$
$$\alpha^2+\beta^2=\left(\alpha+\beta\right)^2-2\alpha\beta$$
$$\left(\alpha-\beta\right)^2=\left(\alpha+\beta\right)^2-4\alpha\beta$$
<br>

$$\left(1-\alpha\right)\left(1-\beta\right)=1-\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\alpha\beta$$
$$\left(2-\alpha\right)\left(2-\beta\right)=4-2\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\alpha\beta$$
<br>

$$\alpha^3+\beta^3=\left(\alpha+\beta\right)^3-3\alpha\beta\left(\alpha+\beta\right)$$
<br>

$$\dfrac{\beta}{\alpha}+\dfrac{\alpha}{\beta}=\dfrac{\alpha^2+\beta^2}{\alpha\beta}$$
$$\dfrac{\beta^2}{\alpha}+\dfrac{\alpha^2}{\beta}=\dfrac{\alpha^3+\beta^3}{\alpha\beta}$$
$$\dfrac{\beta}{\alpha^2}+\dfrac{\alpha}{\beta^2}=\dfrac{\alpha^3+\beta^3}{\left(\alpha\beta\right)^2}$$
<br>

$$\left(\alpha+3\right)+\left(\beta+3\right)=\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+6$$
$$\left(\alpha+3\right)\left(\beta+3\right)=\alpha\beta+3\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+9$$
<br>

### 複素数の範囲で因数分解
$$2x^2-3x+4=2\bigg(x-\dfrac{3+\sqrt{23}i}{4}\bigg)\bigg(x-\dfrac{3-\sqrt{23}i}{4}\bigg)$$
<br>
