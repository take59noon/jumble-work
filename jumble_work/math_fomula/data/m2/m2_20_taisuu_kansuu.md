### 計算式の例

$$y = \log_{4}x$$
$$y = \log_{\frac{1}{4}}x$$
$$y = \log_{2}(x + 1)$$
<br>

$$\log_{2}3,\enspace \log_{3}2,\enspace \log_{4}8$$
<br>

$$\log_{3}x = 3$$
$$x = 3^{3}$$
<br>

$$\log_{\frac{1}{2}}x = 4$$
$$x = \left( \dfrac{1}{2} \right)^{4}$$
<br>


#### 対数方程式
$$\begin{aligned}
& \log_{4}(x + 2) + \log_{4}(x - 4) = 2 \\[.5em]
& (x + 2)(x - 4) = 4^{2}
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& \log_{3}(x - 2) = \log_{9}(x + 10)\\[.5em]
& \log_{3}(x - 2) = \dfrac{\log_{3}(x + 10)}{\log_{3}9}\\[1em]
& 2\log_{3}(x - 2) = \log_{3}(x + 10)\\[.5em]
& (x - 2)^{2} = x + 10\\[.5em]
& x^{2} - 5x - 6 = 0\\[.5em]
& (x + 1)(x - 6) = 0
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

#### 最大・最小  

$$y = \left( \log_{4}x \right)^{2} - \log_{4}x^{2}$$

$t = \log_{4}x$ とすると，

$$y = t^{2} - 2t$$
