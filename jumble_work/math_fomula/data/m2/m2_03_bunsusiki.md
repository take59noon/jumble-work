### 計算式の例
$$\dfrac{x^3-1}{x^2+3x-4}
=\dfrac{(x-1)(x^2+x+1)}{(x+4)(x-1)}
=\dfrac{x^2+x+1}{x+4}$$
<br>

$$\dfrac{a^2+a-6}{a^2-a}\div\dfrac{a^2+5a+6}{a^2+2a}
=\dfrac{(a+3)(a-2)}{a(a-1)}\times\dfrac{a(a+2)}{(a+2)(a+3)}
=\dfrac{a-2}{a-1}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& \dfrac{x+8}{x^2+x-2}+\dfrac{x-4}{x^2-x}=\dfrac{x+8}{(x-1)(x+2)}+\dfrac{x-4}{x(x-1)} \\[1em]
=& \dfrac{x(x+8)+(x-4)(x+2)}{x(x-1)(x+2)}
=\dfrac{(x^2+8x)+(x^2-2x-8)}{x(x-1)(x+2)} \\[1em]
=& \dfrac{2x^2+6x-8}{x(x-1)(x+2)}=\dfrac{2(x^2+3x-4)}{x(x-1)(x+2)} \\[1em]
=& \dfrac{2(x+4)(x-1)}{x(x-1)(x+2)}=\dfrac{2(x+4)}{x(x+2)}
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{x+1}{1-\dfrac{1}{x+2}}+\dfrac{x+3}{1+\dfrac{1}{x+2}}
&= \dfrac{(x+1)(x+2)}{x+2-1}+\dfrac{(x+3)(x+2)}{x+2+1} \cr
&= \dfrac{(x+1)(x+2)}{x+1}+\dfrac{(x+3)(x+2)}{x+3} \\[.8em]
&= (x+2)+(x+2) \cr
&= 2x+4
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& \dfrac{x+3}{x}-\dfrac{x+4}{x+1}-\dfrac{x-6}{x-3}+\dfrac{x-7}{x-4} \\[1em]
=& \dfrac{x+3}{x}-\dfrac{(x+1)+3}{x+1}-\dfrac{(x-3)-3}{x-3}+\dfrac{(x-4)-3}{x-4} \\[1em]
=&\ \left(1+\dfrac{3}{x}\right)-\left(1+\dfrac{3}{x+1}\right)-\left(1-\dfrac{3}{x-3}\right)+\left(1-\dfrac{3}{x-4}\right) \\[1em]
=&\ 3\left(\dfrac{1}{x}-\dfrac{1}{x+1}+\dfrac{1}{x-3}-\dfrac{1}{x-4}\right)=3\left\{\dfrac{(x+1)-x}{x(x+1)}+\dfrac{(x-4)-(x-3)}{(x-3)(x-4)}\right\} \\[1em]
=&\ 3\left\{\dfrac{1}{x(x+1)}-\dfrac{1}{(x-3)(x-4)}\right\}=3\cdot\dfrac{(x-3)(x-4)-x(x+1)}{x(x+1)(x-3)(x-4)} \\[1em]
=&\ 3\cdot\dfrac{-8x+12}{x(x+1)(x-3)(x-4)}=\dfrac{-12(2x-3)}{x(x+1)(x-3)(x-4)}
\end{aligned}$$
