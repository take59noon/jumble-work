### 数式の例
#### 基本式
$$y=\sin{\theta}$$
$$y=\cos{\theta}$$
$$y=\tan{\theta}$$
<br>

#### 値域
$$-1\leqq y\leqq1$$
<br>

#### 周期性・対称性
$$f\left(x+p\right)=f\left(x\right)$$
$$f\left(-x\right)=-f\left(x\right)$$
$$f\left(-x\right)=f\left(x\right)$$
<br>

#### グラフの式
$$y=\sin{\left(\theta-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)}$$
$$y=\cos{\left(\theta-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)}$$
$$y=\tan{\left(\theta-\dfrac{\pi}{3}\right)}$$
<br>

$$y=2\sin{\left(2\theta+\dfrac{2}{3}\pi\right)}+1$$
$$y=2\cos{\left(2\theta+\dfrac{2}{3}\pi\right)}+1$$
$$y=2\tan{\left(2\theta+\dfrac{2}{3}\pi\right)}+1$$
<br>
