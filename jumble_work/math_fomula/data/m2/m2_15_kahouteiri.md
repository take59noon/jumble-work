### 加法定理
$$\sin{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}$$
$$\sin{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta}$$
<br>

$$\cos{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\cos{\alpha}\cos{\beta}-\sin{\alpha}\sin{\beta}$$
$$\cos{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\cos{\alpha}\cos{\beta}+\sin{\alpha}\sin{\beta}$$
<br>

$$\tan{\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}=\dfrac{\tan{\alpha}+\tan{\beta}}{1-\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}$$
$$\tan{\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}=\dfrac{\tan{\alpha}-\tan{\beta}}{1+\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}$$
<br>

### なす角
$$\tan{\theta}=\left|\dfrac{\tan{\alpha}-\tan{\beta}}{1+\tan{\alpha}\tan{\beta}}\right|=\left|\dfrac{m_1-m_2}{1+m_1m_2}\right|$$
<br>

### ２倍角の公式
$$\sin{2\alpha}=2\sin{\alpha}\cos{\alpha}$$
$$\begin{aligned}
\cos{2\alpha}
&=\cos^2{\alpha}-\sin^2{\alpha} \cr
&=2\cos^2{\alpha}-1 \cr
&=1-2\sin^2{\alpha}
\end{aligned}$$
$$\tan{2\alpha}=\dfrac{2\tan{\alpha}}{1-\tan^2{\alpha}}$$
<br>

### ３倍角の公式
$$\sin{3\alpha}=3\sin{\alpha}-4\sin^3{\alpha}$$
$$\cos{3\alpha}=-3\cos{\alpha}+4\cos^3{\alpha}$$
<br>

### 半角の公式
$$\sin^2{\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}=\dfrac{1-\cos{\alpha}}{2}$$
$$\cos^2{\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}=\dfrac{1+\cos{\alpha}}{2}$$
$$\tan^2{\dfrac{\alpha}{2}}=\dfrac{1-\cos{\alpha}}{1+\cos{\alpha}}$$
