### 計算式の例
$$y=4^x$$
$$y=\left(\dfrac{1}{4}\right)^x$$
<br>

$$2^\frac{1}{2},\enspace 4^\frac{1}{4},\enspace 8^\frac{1}{8}$$
$$\sqrt2,\enspace \sqrt[3]{3},\enspace \sqrt[6]{6}$$
<br>


$$2^{2x+1}-5\cdot2^x-12=0$$

$2^x=t$ とする．  

$$\begin{aligned}
& 2\cdot(2^x)^2-5\cdot2^x-12=0 \cr
& 2t^2-5t-12=0 \cr
& (2t+3)(t-4)=0
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\left(\dfrac{1}{9}\right)^x-\dfrac{1}{3^x}-6=0$$

$\left(\dfrac{1}{3}\right)^x=t$ とする．  

$$\begin{aligned}
& \left\{\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^x\right\}^2-\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^x-6=0 \\[.8em]
& t^2-t-6=0 \cr
& (t+2)(t-3)=0
\end{aligned}$$
