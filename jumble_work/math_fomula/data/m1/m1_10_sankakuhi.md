### 三角比
$$
\sin{\theta}=\dfrac{y}{r}, \enspace
\cos{\theta}=\dfrac{x}{r}, \enspace
\tan{\theta}=\dfrac{y}{x}
$$
<br>

$$
y=r\sin{\theta}, \enspace
x=r\cos{\theta}, \enspace
y=x\tan{\theta}
$$
<br>

### 三角比の値
$$
\sin{30\degree}=\dfrac{1}{2},\enspace 
\cos{30\degree}=\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\enspace
\tan{30\degree}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}
$$
$$
\sin{45\degree}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}},\enspace
\cos{45\degree}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}, \enspace
\tan{45\degree}=1
$$
$$
\sin{60\degree}=\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\enspace
\cos{60\degree}=\dfrac{1}{2},\enspace
\tan{60\degree}=\sqrt{3}
$$
<br>

### 三角比の変換
* $90\degree - \theta$
$$\sin{(90\degree-\theta)}=\cos\theta$$
$$\cos{(90\degree-\theta)}=\sin\theta$$
$$\tan{(90\degree-\theta)}=\dfrac{1}{\tan\theta}$$
<br>

* $180\degree - \theta$
$$\sin{(180\degree-\theta)}=\sin\theta$$
$$\cos{(180\degree-\theta)}=-\cos\theta$$
$$\tan{(180\degree-\theta)}=-\tan\theta$$
<br>

* $90\degree + \theta$
$$\sin{(90\degree+\theta)}=\cos\theta$$
$$\cos{(90\degree+\theta)}=-\sin\theta$$
$$\tan{(90\degree+\theta)}=-\dfrac{1}{\tan\theta}$$
