### 三角比の相互関係
$$\tan{\theta}=\dfrac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}}$$
$$\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}=1$$
$$1+\tan^2{\theta}=\dfrac{1}{\cos^2{\theta}}$$
<br>

### 相互関係の例
* $\sin{\theta}=\dfrac{4}{5}$ のとき  
$$\cos{\theta}=\sqrt{1-\sin^2{\theta}}=\sqrt{1-\left(\dfrac{4}{5}\right)^2}
=\sqrt{\dfrac{9}{25}}=\dfrac{3}{5}$$
$$\tan{\theta}=\dfrac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}}=\dfrac{4}{5}\div\dfrac{3}{5}=\dfrac{4}{3}$$
<br>

* $\tan{\theta}=3$ のとき  
$$\cos^2{\theta}=\dfrac{1}{1+\tan^2{\theta}}=\dfrac{1}{1+3^2}=\dfrac{1}{10}$$
$$\cos{\theta}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{10}}$$
$$\sin{\theta}=\tan{\theta}\cos{\theta}=3\cdot\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{10}}=\dfrac{3}{\sqrt{10}}$$
<br>

### $2$ 乗の展開  
$$\begin{aligned}
& \left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)^2+\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)^2 \cr
=\ &\left(\sin^2{\theta}+2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right)+\left(\sin^2{\theta}-2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right) \cr
=\ & 2\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right) \cr
=\ & 2
\end{aligned}$$

### 対称式
#### $2$ 次式
$$2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}=\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)^2-\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}\right)$$
$$\left(\sin{\theta}-\cos{\theta}\right)^2=\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}-2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}$$
<br>

#### $3$ 次式
$$\sin^3{\theta}+\cos^3{\theta}=\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)^3-3\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)$$
$$\sin^3{\theta}+\cos^3{\theta}=\left(\sin{\theta}+\cos{\theta}\right)\left(\sin^2{\theta}+\cos^2{\theta}-\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}\right)$$
