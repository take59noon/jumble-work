<!-- ## 根号を含む式の計算 -->
### 有理化
$$\dfrac{k}{\sqrt a}=\dfrac{k\sqrt a}{a}$$
$$\dfrac{k}{\sqrt a+\sqrt b}=\dfrac{k \big(\sqrt a-\sqrt b\big)}{a-b}$$
$$\dfrac{k}{\sqrt a-\sqrt b}=\dfrac{k \big(\sqrt a+\sqrt b\big)}{a-b}$$
<br>

### 計算式の例
$$\sqrt2\big(3\sqrt6+\sqrt{50}\big)=6\sqrt3+10$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\big(\sqrt2+\sqrt3\big)^2 
&=\big(\sqrt2\big)^2+2\cdot\sqrt2\cdot\sqrt3+\big(\sqrt3\big)^2 \cr
&=2+2\sqrt6+3 \cr
&=5+2\sqrt6
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\big(\sqrt5-\sqrt2\big)^2
&=\big(\sqrt5\big)^2-2\cdot\sqrt5\cdot\sqrt2+\big(\sqrt2\big)^2 \cr
&=5-2\sqrt{10}+2 \cr
&=7-2\sqrt{10}
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\big(\sqrt3+5\big)\big(\sqrt3-5\big)
&=\big(\sqrt3\big)^2-5^2 \cr
&=3-25
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{1}{\sqrt2+\sqrt5-\sqrt7}
&=\dfrac{\sqrt2+\sqrt5+\sqrt7}{\big\{\big(\sqrt2+\sqrt5\big)-\sqrt7\big\}\big\{\big(\sqrt2+\sqrt5\big)+\sqrt7\big\}} \cr
&=\dfrac{\sqrt2+\sqrt5+\sqrt7}{\big(\sqrt2+\sqrt5\big)^2-\big(\sqrt7\big)^2} \cr
&=\dfrac{\sqrt2+\sqrt5+\sqrt7}{2\sqrt{10}} \cr
&=\dfrac{2\sqrt5+5\sqrt2+\sqrt{70}}{20} \cr
\end{aligned}$$
