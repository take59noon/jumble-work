### ２次関数のグラフ
$$y=a\left(x-p\right)^2+q$$
$$\textrm{軸は直線 $x=p$，頂点は点$\left(p,\enspace q\right)$}$$
<br>

### 平方完成
$$\begin{aligned}
y
&=ax^2+bx+c \cr
&=a\bigg(x^2+\dfrac{b}{a}x\bigg)+c \cr
&=a\bigg\{\bigg(x+\dfrac{b}{2a}\bigg)^2-\bigg(\dfrac{b}{2a}\bigg)^2\bigg\}+c \\[.8em]
&=a\bigg(x+\dfrac{b}{2a}\bigg)^2-\dfrac{b^2-4ac}{4a}
\end{aligned}$$
$$\textrm{軸は直線 $x=-\dfrac{b}{2a}$，頂点は点$\left(-\dfrac{b}{2a},\enspace -\dfrac{b^2-4ac}{4a}\right)$}$$
<br>

#### 平方完成の例
$$\begin{aligned}
y
&=3x^2+5x+1 \\[.1em]
&=3\bigg(x^2+\dfrac{5}{3}x\bigg)+1 \\[.8em]
&=3\bigg\{\bigg(x+\dfrac{5}{6}\bigg)^2-\bigg(\dfrac{5}{6}\bigg)^2\bigg\}+1 \\[.8em]
&=3\bigg(x+\dfrac{5}{6}\bigg)^2-\dfrac{13}{12}
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
y
&=-2x^2+6x-1 \\[.1em]
&=-2\left(x^2-3x\right)-1 \\[.1em]
&=-2\bigg\{\bigg(x-\dfrac{3}{2}\bigg)^2-\bigg(\dfrac{3}{2}\bigg)^2\bigg\}-1 \\[.8em]
&=-2\bigg(x-\dfrac{3}{2}\bigg)^2+\dfrac{7}{2}
\end{aligned}$$
