### 平均値
$$\overline{x}=\dfrac{1}{n}\left(x_1+x_2+\cdots+x_n\right)$$
<br>

#### 平均値の計算例
$$\overline{x}=\dfrac{1}{30}\left(10\times1+15\times5+20\times13+25\times9+30\times2\right)=\dfrac{630}{30}=21$$
<br>

### 分散
$$s^2=\dfrac{1}{n}\left\{\left(x_1-\overline{x}\right)^2+\left(x_2-\overline{x}\right)^2+\cdots+\left(x_n-\overline{x}\right)^2\right\}$$

$$s^2=\overline{x^2}-\left(\overline{x}\right)^2$$
<br>

### 標準偏差
$$s=\sqrt{\dfrac{1}{n}\left\{\left(x_1-\overline{x}\right)^2+\left(x_2-\overline{x}\right)^2+\cdots+\left(x_n-\overline{x}\right)^2\right\}}$$
<br>

#### 変量の変換
$$\overline{y}=a\overline{x}+b$$
$${s_y}^2=a^2{s_x}^2$$
$$s_y=\left|a\right|s_x$$
<br>

### 共分散
$$s_{xy}=\dfrac{1}{n}\left\{\left(x_1-\overline{x}\right)\left(y_1-\overline{y}\right)+\left(x_2-\overline{x}\right)\left(y_2-\overline{y}\right)+\cdots+\left(x_n-\overline{x}\right)\left(y_n-\overline{y}\right)\right\}$$
