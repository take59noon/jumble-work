### 計算式の例
$$\begin{aligned}
\dfrac{4}{3}x+\dfrac{1}{6}x &> \dfrac{1}{2}x-\dfrac{3}{2} \\[0.5em]
8x+x &> 3x-9 
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
0.5x+3 &\leqq 5-0.3x \\[0.3em]
5x+30 &\leqq 50-3x
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{cases}
3x+1 < 4(x-2) \\[0.5em]
\dfrac{x-5}{4} \geqq \dfrac{2x+3}{10}
\end{cases}$$
<br>

### 絶対値記号を含む不等式
$$\begin{aligned}
& \left|x-3\right|<8 \cr
& -8<x-3<8 \cr
& -5<x<11 
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& \left|x-3\right|>8 \cr
& x-3<-8, \enspace 8<x-3 \cr
& x<-5, \enspace 11<x
\end{aligned}$$
<br>

$$\begin{aligned}
& \left|x-3\right|>8 \cr
& x-3<-8 \enspace\textrm{または}\enspace x-3>8 \cr
& x<-5 \enspace\textrm{または}\enspace x>11 
\end{aligned}$$
